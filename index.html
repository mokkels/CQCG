Donald Trump: Life Timeline

1946

Born on June 14 in Queens, New York.

1968

Graduates from Wharton; joins Trump Organization.

1976

Opens Grand Hyatt Hotel in Manhattan.

1982

Opens Trump Tower on Fifth Avenue.

1990

Opens Trump Taj Mahal; faces $1B debt crisis.

2004

Debuts "The Apprentice" on NBC.

2016

Elected 45th U.S. President.

2020

Loses re-election to Joe Biden.

2024

Elected 47th U.S. President; convicted on 34 felony counts.
